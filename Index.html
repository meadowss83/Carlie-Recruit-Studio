Instagram strategy

  • Reels over photosReels get 3–5× the reach. A 15-second drill clip beats any photo.
  • Bio = recruiting resume"PG | 6'0" | 19 PPG | #ClassOf2027" — coaches scan bios in 3 seconds.
  • Hashtags in first commentPut 8–12 tags in the first comment, not the caption. Cleaner look.
  • Tag school + AAU teamMultiplies reach to coaches already following those accounts.

Platform focus

  • Twitter/X first — coaches live hereMost D1/D2 coaches search prospects on Twitter. Stat tweets, tournament results, visit announcements — this moves the needle most directly.
  • Hudl second — it's her resumeEvery coach she contacts clicks Hudl before responding. Current film and bio is non-negotiable.
  • Instagram third — brand + characterCoaches check Instagram to understand who she is as a person, not just a player. Reels, stories, how she talks about teammates.
  • TikTok — revisit laterHigh effort, lowest recruiting ROI right now. Once the core 3 are running consistently, add it if she wants broader reach.

Twitter/X strategy

  • This is where coaches liveMost D1/D2 coaches are very active here. Highest priority platform.
  • Retweet school athleticsKeeps you in coaches' feeds on non-game days.
  • Follow target school coachesMany check who follows them — it signals real interest.
  • Announce visits publicly"Excited to visit [school] this weekend" gets coach engagement.

Hudl / NCSA strategy

  • Update reel monthlyCoaches want recent form — not highlights from last year.
  • First 90 seconds decide everythingBest play goes at 0:00. Coaches often stop at 1:30.
  • Add full game filmCoaches want to see full possessions, decisions, off-ball movement.
  • NCSA = digital recruiting resumeGPA, stats, video, test scores — keep it current at all times.

The big picture

  • Consistency over virality3× a week for 12 months beats going viral once. Coaches track patterns.
  • Academic posts matterHonor roll, AP classes — coaches love student-athlete content.
  • Character shows through contentHow she credits teammates and handles losses matters to coaches.
  • DM coaches directlyA short genuine DM linking her Hudl is completely normal and welcomed.
